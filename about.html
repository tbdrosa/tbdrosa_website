Biography

Hi! Welcome to my portfolio. I’d like to break away from the whole third person shtick. I prefer using my own words to share my thoughts and experience. My name is John Anthony Rosa I’m a self-taught photographer and web-developer from New York City.

Ever since I was a child I’ve had a huge passion for learning and creating art, whether that involved taking apart items in my house and building something or reading anything I could get my hands on.

If I am not out with my friends creating art then I am busy in the library studying for my next exam. And If I am not in the library – then I am probably lost in a bookstore corner reading.

Expertise

My work focuses on mostly on portraits and my goal when creating art is to capture the moment that I genuinely hope makes you feel something when you look at my images. Pairing my cheap lenses with natural light gives my photographs a soft pastel feel.
