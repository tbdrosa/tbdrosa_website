Dear Friend,

It's been a while. Thought I’d post a throwback. to June 28, 2018. Hard to imagine it’s been four years since these images were taken. Almost seems as if they were taken last summer, if I’m being totally honest with myself. Perhaps this has something to do with COVID situation and many of us have lost the concept of time since we spend majority of our days in doors in the comfort of our homes.

I remember catching up with some of my friends and managed to squeeze in a photoshoot before I met up with them. Right away we reminisced of our college experience and what we hoped to accomplish once we are done with school. Happy to say Amber is doing very well now. At the time she had a poetry blog that I really enjoyed reading from time to when when she would post. If you are interested in what she’s doing now feel free to see her work at her site here.

“Wouldn’t it be funny if we took a photo right in the middle of the Boardwalk.
Having everyone stare at us.”
